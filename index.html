About Me

"Adventure is out there!"

I know that it might be a cliched statment, but I love adventure and the opportunity to explore the amazing world that it out there. Whether it be learning new skills such as coding, experiencing great food in cities around the world or capturing the amazing sites that the great outdoors has to offer I am always up for fun new experiences.

I have a passion for creativity and love having any opportunity to express that creativity in my work. I have background in marketing, digital design, photography and videography and am always looking for opportunities to add to my creative skillset. When it comes to creative work I love to be able to try my hand at just about anything.

I graduated from the University of Utah in 2018 with a Bachelor of Science in Communication, specifically focusing on Strategic Communication, and a minor in Design. Since graduating I have worked for various start-up companies helping with the initial implementation of a marketing strategy. I have also had the opportunity to work on the creation of digital content for a variety of companies.

Whether it be creating or managing digital assets for a marketing strategy or photographing amazing landscapes in the great outdoors, I am always up for an adventure.
